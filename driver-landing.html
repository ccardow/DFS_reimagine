Frequently Asked Questions

So, how much money will I make?

Once on-boarded, we will provide you with a pre-mapped, optimized delivery route for the day. The amount of money you earn depends on a few factors, such as the distance and time it takes to complete your deliveries. 

When will I get paid?

You will be paid directly into your bank account on a weekly basis. 

What are the requirements?

To qualify, you must have a valid driver’s license, vehicle insurance, registration and pass a background check. We also require you to have a smartphone, data plan, phone charger, a reliable vehicle with working air conditioner and a 12v power outlet fuse for the provided electric cooler bags. In addition, you must follow all COVID related protocols and be certified to serve alcohol in the delivery areas.  

Please note we may require additional documentation to prove your eligibility to work in Canada.

How soon can I start?

Once the sign up and validation process are complete, a member of our team will contact you with your pre-optimized delivery route, training material and any other necessary equipment, i.e. refrigerated bags etc.
